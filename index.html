What it is

Pill Tracker is a simple, private, offline-friendly app for managing daily medications, logging doses, and tracking the health metrics that matter — blood pressure, weight, mood, symptoms.

It's built for everyday use, whether you take one pill or a dozen, whether you manage your own medications or help an aging parent track theirs.

Why it's different

  • Privacy-first — medication names never appear on your lock-screen notifications.
  • Offline-friendly — log doses and record metrics even without a connection; data syncs when you're back online.
  • Your data stays yours — optional cloud backup goes to your own Google Drive or Dropbox, not our servers.
  • No ads, no trackers, no selling of data.
